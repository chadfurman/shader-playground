+ Secret Ingredient: Journey's Multiplayer

Anonymous. No voice, no text, no usernames. You see another player's evolving flame drifting alongside yours. Get close and your genomes cross-pollinate — your flames temporarily blend. The only communication is proximity and beauty. You don't know who they are until the session ends.

Game Theory: The Scoring Question

Jenova Chen (creator of Flower, Journey, fl0w) wrote his entire MFA thesis on flow state in games. His key finding: intrinsic motivation beats extrinsic. Games without scores (Flower, Journey, Minecraft creative mode) drive deeper engagement for regular players.

The answer: layer it. Core experience is pure zen/flow — no score, just beauty. Then optional layers for players who want them: genome diversity score, aesthetic ratings, leaderboards. Audiosurf did exactly this with its separate "zen mode."
