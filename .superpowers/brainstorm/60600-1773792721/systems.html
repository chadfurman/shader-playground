Flight & Navigation

The player's body in the world

You are a point of light — a flame seed — drifting through fractal space. The camera follows you in third-person, close enough to feel immersed, far enough to see the world blooming around you.

Three input modes to prototype:

DRIFT

Gentle analog steering. Tilt, float, carried by wind. Flower energy. Low intensity.

TUNNEL

Fast, forward motion. Weave and hook. Dyad energy. High intensity synesthesia rush.

GESTURAL

Shape and sculpt. Wave, draw, conduct. Fantasia energy. Creative, expressive.

Build all three. Play all three. Keep what feels like magic. Maybe all three become game modes.

Taste Model

The world learns you

Inherited: IGMM clustering, 74-dimension feature vectors, favorite profiles, variation frequency analysis, palette feature extraction.

New: In shader-playground, taste is fed by explicit votes (up/down). In Spanda, taste is fed by behavior. Lingering near a flame is a vote. Chasing a mutation is a vote. Turning away is a vote. The game reads your body language — your movement patterns become preference data. Over a session, the universe subtly reshapes itself around your aesthetic unconscious. No two players' Spanda looks the same after 10 minutes.

Multiplayer: Silent Communion

Journey model — anonymous, beautiful, optional

You're drifting through your Spanda. A second light appears. You don't know who they are. There's no voice chat, no text, no username. Just a flame with a different genome — a different taste, a different aesthetic fingerprint.

Proximity = cross-pollination. Get close and your genomes blend. The world between you becomes a child of both tastes. Something neither of you would have created alone.

Session end = reveal. When you leave, you see their name. That's it. A quiet "we were here together."

Monetization: Base game is single-player ($10-15). Optional subscription ($2-3/mo) enables multiplayer — keeps servers alive without ads, microtransactions, or compromise.
